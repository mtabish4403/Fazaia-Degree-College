About Us

Fazaia Degree College Peshawar

Who We Are

Fazaia Degree College Peshawar is one of the leading educational institutions operating under the prestigious Pakistan Air Force (PAF). Known for excellence, discipline, and holistic development, the college stands as a symbol of quality education in the region.

The college has progressed through multiple stages of academic evolution, becoming a respected degree-awarding institution that serves both PAF families and civilians.

Our Journey

  • 1960: Established as Peshawar Model School.
  • 1967: Upgraded to Intermediate Level.
  • 1981: Achieved Degree College Status.
  • Today: A premier institution offering modern education from intermediate to bachelor's levels.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a center of educational excellence where discipline, integrity, and innovation empower students to become future leaders.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education in a disciplined and intellectually stimulating environment, focusing on character building, academic excellence, and leadership development.

Affiliation with Pakistan Air Force

Fazaia Degree College Peshawar is part of the Fazaia chain of educational institutions operated by the Pakistan Air Force. These institutions are known for their strong academic standards, discipline, and commitment to producing responsible citizens who uphold values of integrity, respect, and service to the nation.
